Pasta Primavera with Creamy Garlic Cashew Sauce

Delicious vegan pasta primavera packed with tender vegetables and tossed in a creamy garlic cashew sauce. This easy vegan pasta primavera recipe has wonderful flavors and plenty of protein and healthy fats for a comforting plant-based meal the whole family will love!

    Veggies:

  • 2 cups Broccoli florets, small
  • 1 Carrot, large
  • 1 1/2 cups Cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 tsp Onion powder
  • 1 Red bell pepper, medium
  • 1/2 Red onion, medium
  • 2 Zucchini, medium

    Condiments:

  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice, fresh

    Pasta and cereals:

  • 12 oz Penne pasta

    Powder ingredients:

  • 1 Black pepper, Freshly ground
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 Red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Salt and black pepper

    Oils and vinegars:

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil

    Walnuts and seeds:

  • 3/4 cup Cashews, raw

    Bread and oven:

  • 1 Garlic costini/garlic bread

    Liquids:

  • 1/2 cup Water
Spicy Cashew Chickpea Broccoli Salad with Ginger Peanut Dressing

Delicious healthy Thai-inspired broccoli salad made with colorful veggies like crunchy carrots, red cabbage and sweet bell pepper. This easy vegan cashew broccoli salad is tossed in a spicy ginger peanut dressing and packed with delicious, plant based protein thanks to chickpeas!

    Veggies:

  • 1 medium head Broccoli
  • 1 cup Carrots
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup Cilantro, fresh
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 tsp Ginger, fresh
  • 1/3 cup Green onions
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 2 cups Red cabbage, packed

    Condiments:

  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1/4 cup Peanut butter, natural creamy

    Oils and vinegars:

  • 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil

    Walnuts and seeds:

  • 2 1/2 tbsp Gluten free soy sauce or coconut aminos, low sodium

    Liquids:

  • 2 tbsp Water

    Other:

  • ½ cup roasted cashew halves (honey roasted or a fun flavor is yummy)

    Fruits & Veggies:

  • 1/3 cup Blueberries, fresh or frozen
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger, ground
  • 1 tbsp Sage, fresh
  • 1/2 tsp Thyme, dried

    Condiments:

  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup, pure

    Oils and vinegars:

  • 1 tsp Coconut oil or olive oil

    Powder ingredients:

  • 1/4 tsp Allspice
  • 1 Black pepper, Freshly ground
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 tsp Salt

    Walnuts & seeds:

  • 1/2 tsp Cumin, ground
The Best Healthy Turkey Lasagna You'll Ever Eat

The BEST turkey lasagna ever. This healthy turkey lasagna recipe is made with a homemade tomato basil sauce packed with fresh herbs & Italian spices, lean ground turkey, mozzarella, creamy ricotta and a touch of parmesan. Perfect for serving crowds, family-style dinners, or freezing for later!

    Meat:

  • 1 lb 94% lean ground turkey, lean

    Pasta & eggs:

  • 10 Whole wheat or gluten free lasagna noodles
  • 1 Egg

    Veggies:

  • 1/2 cup Basil, fresh
  • 1/4 cup Basil, packed fresh
  • 1 Basil, Fresh
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 2 tsp Oregano, dried
  • 1 (28 ounce) can Tomatoes
  • 1 White onion

    Condiments:

  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup, pure
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Tomato sauce

    Oils and vinegars:

  • 1 tsp Olive oil

    Powder ingredients:

  • 2 Black pepper, Freshly ground
  • 1 tsp Fennel seeds
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt

    Walnuts & seeds:

  • 1/2 tsp Cumin, ground

    Dairy:

  • 3/4 cup Parmesan, grated
  • 1 (15 ounce) container Ricotta, part skim
  • 16 oz Whole milk mozzarella, fresh slices thin

    Liquids:

  • 1/2 cup Water

Enjoy! ✨
